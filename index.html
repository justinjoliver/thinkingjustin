Me

An aspiring deep thinker and a shamelessly proud alumnus of the University of Michigan, I study how the world works through logic, patterns, and numbers.

Professionally, I lead statistical and economic analysis, providing insights and consulting best analytical practices to make better quantitatively-driven decisions. From a work at the Federal Reserve Bank of Dallas, I worked on a peer-reviewed academic research in economics with Soojin Jo (Bank of Canada).

Deep thinkers need downtime too, to help them dream up big thoughts. So I still finds time for photography, cycling, and time running sweating over complex issues outdoor.
